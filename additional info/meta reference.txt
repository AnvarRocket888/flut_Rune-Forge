OVERUNE FORGING - APP STORE DESCRIPTION

APP NAME: OveRune Forging

SUBTITLE: Collect Runes and Forge Spells

DESCRIPTION:
Every hour a new rune appears, waiting to be collected. Fire, water, earth, air, spirit — each one carries its own element, rarity, and story. Build your collection, then put it to work: combine runes in the Forge to create spells. Fifteen recipes to discover, and any unknown combination becomes a mystery spell of its own.
As you collect and forge, your tower grows floor by floor. Upgrade runes with energy that recovers over time, climb from Apprentice to Legendary Runesmith, unlock achievements, and earn trophies. Rune Forge gives you one hour at a time and lets you build something entirely your own.
