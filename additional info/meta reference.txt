DESCRIPTION:
Every night you go to sleep, you start building a tower. Each hour of rest adds one floor. Sleep the full eight hours and your tower stands tall and complete. Wake up early and you see exactly how many floors you missed.
The idea is simple. Good sleep should feel like progress, not a chore. Instead of charts and numbers, you watch a colorful tower grow night after night. Each floor has its own name and color, from the Foundation Stone at the bottom to the Golden Crown at the top.
